Presentation Sharing

After placing a point-to-point video call or initiating a multipoint conference, you can share your desktop or application with the remote site.

Before presentation sharing, ensure that Presentation is set to Enable under > Call and Presentation.

The following uses the video call window as an example to describe how to start and stop sharing presentation.

In the video call window, click and choose Share Presentation to share your computer desktop, as shown in Figure 1. For details about how to place a video call, see Placing a Point-to-Point Call.

Alternatively, click and choose Share Application to share a running application.

To stop the sharing, click .
