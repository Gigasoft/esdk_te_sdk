TE Desktop Online Help

Welcome to online help for the Telepresence Endpoint (TE) Desktop. Click the following links to browse desired topics:

  • Basic Functions
    This topic describes the functions of the TE Desktop.
  • Downloading and Installing
    This topic describes how to download and install the TE Desktop.
  • Logging In and Out
    This topic describes how to set required parameters before using the TE Desktop and how to log in to and out of the TE Desktop.
  • Local Contacts
    This topic describes how to create, search, import, export local contacts and create group contacts.
  • Corporate Directory
    This topic describes how to search a contact, call the contact, and add the contact to the local directory.
  • Placing a Point-to-Point Call
    The TE Desktop support placing a point-to-point call through video or audio.
  • Multipoint Conference
    The TE Desktop can initiate a multipoint conference or join a multipoint conference using the conference ID or link.
  • Conference Control
    In a multipoint conference, you can request chair control rights and manage the sites.
  • Presentation Sharing
    After placing a point-to-point video call or initiating a multipoint conference, you can share your desktop or application with the remote site.
  • Audiovisual Tests
    Before a conference, you can perform audiovisual tests to check the status of audio and video devices.
  • Settings
    You can configure personal info, system, and advanced settings.
  • Technical Specifications
    This topic describes the technical specifications of the TE Desktop.
