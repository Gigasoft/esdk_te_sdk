Placing a Point-to-Point Call

The TE Desktop support placing a point-to-point call through video or audio.

The following modes are available to place calls:
  • Click . In the displayed local contacts list, select a contact, then click to place an audio call or to place a video call.
  • Click to open Corporate Directory, search the contact and select Place Video Call or Place Audio Call.
  • Click . In the displayed dialer, enter the number to you want to dial and click to place an audio call or to place a video call.
  • Click . In the displayed call records, select a record, and click to place an audio call or to place a video call.

A call window is then displayed. To cancel the call, click .

  • During an audio call, click to request to switch the call to a video call.
  • During a video call, click > to switch the call to an audio call.
    NOTE:
    • If presentation is sharing in a video call, stop the presentation and switch the video call to an audio call.
    • After a video call is set up, you can perform all operations described in Icons in the Call Window in the call window, such as muting/unmuting the local speaker, muting/unmuting the microphone, and starting/stopping sharing presentation.
